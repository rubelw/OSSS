This public hearing will be labeled 'Public Hearing A' for the District's budget process. This is the time and place for the public hearing on the matter of the proposed tax levy for the 2026-27 fiscal year as provided in the notices mailed to taxpayers."
President Carpenter states: "Are there any questions, comments, or objections from those present?"
President Carpenter asks: "Michelle Wearmouth, as Board Secretary, have any questions, comments, or objections been filed in writing?"
President Carpenter states: "I declare the hearing for Public Tax Notices for the property tax levy for the 2026-27 fiscal year to be closed."
